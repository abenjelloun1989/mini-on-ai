# Freelance Service Tier Pricing Architecture Swipe File

> Provides freelance consultants and creatives with 10 real-structure examples of three-tier service packaging so they can anchor client choices and increase average project value.

---

### 1. Brand Identity — Essentials to Full System

**STARTER — $1,200**
Logo design (3 concepts, 2 revision rounds) + brand color palette + typography selection. Delivered as PNG, SVG, and PDF files. Ideal for solopreneurs launching fast.

**GROWTH — $2,800**
Everything in Starter + brand guidelines document (12–15 pages), business card design, email signature, and social media profile kit (6 sized assets). Ideal for small businesses building a cohesive look.

**AUTHORITY — $5,500**
Everything in Growth + brand story/messaging framework, letterhead + invoice template, brand photography art direction brief, pitch deck template (10 slides), and a 60-minute brand strategy kickoff call. Ideal for funded startups or rebrands.

*Use this when pitching brand identity to early-stage businesses. The naming (Starter/Growth/Authority) matches where clients see themselves psychologically. The middle tier is intentionally loaded with value to anchor most clients there.*

---

### 2. Copywriting — Website Package Tiers

**CORE — $900**
Copywriting for 3 website pages (Home, About, Services). Up to 400 words per page. One revision round. Delivered in a Google Doc formatted for easy paste-in.

**CONVERSION — $1,900**
Copywriting for 6 pages (Home, About, Services, FAQ, Contact, one Blog post). Includes SEO keyword research for each page, meta descriptions, and two revision rounds. Light brand voice questionnaire included.

**FULL FUNNEL — $3,800**
Everything in Conversion + sales/landing page copy, 3-email welcome sequence, case study write-up (one existing client interview), and a 90-day content calendar outline. Two strategy calls included. Three revision rounds.

*Perfect for web designers who want to offer copy as an add-on or for copywriters pitching website redesign support. The 'Full Funnel' tier captures clients who are serious about launch, not just going live.*

### 3. Social Media Management — Monthly Retainer

**PRESENCE — $750/mo**
12 posts per month across 2 platforms. Captions, hashtags, and static graphic templates. Monthly performance summary. No paid ad management.

**MOMENTUM — $1,500/mo**
20 posts per month across 3 platforms. Includes 4 Reels/short-form video scripts (client films, we edit captions). Community management (comments/DMs, Mon–Fri). Monthly strategy call (30 min).

**DOMINATE — $3,000/mo**
Everything in Momentum + 8 Reels with full editing, paid ad management (up to $2,000 ad spend), bi-weekly strategy calls, monthly analytics report with actionable recommendations, and priority Slack access.

*Use for social media management proposals. The 'Dominate' tier includes paid ads, which is a natural upgrade path. Naming escalates from passive to active to aggressive — matches ambitious business owner psychology.*

---

### 4. UX/UI Design — App or SaaS Product

**FOUNDATION — $4,500**
UX audit of existing product or competitive analysis (if new). Wireframes for up to 8 key screens. One round of user flow review. Figma file delivery.

**BUILD — $9,500**
Everything in Foundation + high-fidelity UI design for up to 20 screens, interactive prototype, component library (design system basics), and developer handoff file with annotations.

**LAUNCH-READY — $18,000**
Everything in Build + full design system (50+ components), user testing coordination (3–5 participants, we synthesize findings), two rounds of iteration post-testing, onboarding flow design, and weekly check-ins throughout 10-week engagement.

*Positions you as a product partner, not just a screen painter. The jump from Build to Launch-Ready is justified by user testing and the design system — both of which reduce developer costs downstream, making the ROI argument easy.*

### 5. SEO Services — Monthly Tiered Retainer

**LOCAL — $600/mo**
Google Business Profile optimization, 4 locally targeted blog posts per month, basic on-page SEO for up to 5 existing pages, monthly keyword ranking report.

**REGIONAL — $1,400/mo**
Everything in Local + technical SEO audit + fixes (first month), 8 blog posts per month, backlink outreach (5 targets/mo), competitor gap analysis quarterly.

**NATIONAL — $3,200/mo**
Everything in Regional + 12 long-form content pieces per month, PR-driven link building (10+ targets/mo), Core Web Vitals optimization, schema markup implementation, and dedicated account manager with weekly reporting.

*Use with brick-and-mortar businesses or e-commerce brands. Tier names reflect client ambition geography, which resonates immediately. The 'National' tier's backlink and PR work is what actually commands the price — make sure to explain this verbally.*

### 6. Video Production — Corporate or Brand Video

**ESSENTIAL — $2,500**
Up to 2-hour shoot (single location), 1 edited video (60–90 seconds), basic color grade, licensed background music, one revision. MP4 delivery.

**SIGNATURE — $5,500**
Up to full-day shoot (2 locations), 2 edited videos (hero video up to 3 min + 60-sec social cut), professional color grade, custom music licensing, motion title cards, two revisions. Includes pre-production call and shot list.

**CAMPAIGN — $11,000**
Everything in Signature + pre-production scriptwriting, 2-day shoot (up to 3 locations), 5 video deliverables (hero, 3 social cuts, 1 testimonial), closed captions, YouTube/LinkedIn optimization, and a 30-day paid amplification strategy doc.

*Video production tiers work well because the deliverable count is visible and tangible. The jump to Campaign tier makes sense for product launches or annual brand refreshes. Quote in person if possible — video clients often upgrade on-the-spot when they see the scope.*

### 7. Executive Resume + LinkedIn — Career Pivot Package

**REFRESH — $350**
Resume rewrite (up to 2 pages) optimized for ATS and your target role. One revision round. 3-business-day turnaround.

**REBRAND — $650**
Everything in Refresh + LinkedIn profile rewrite (headline, about section, experience bullets), keyword optimization for recruiter search, and a 30-minute strategy call to align on positioning.

**FULL PIVOT — $1,100**
Everything in Rebrand + cover letter template (customizable for 3 role types), 30/60/90-day job search action plan, interview talking points document, and email templates for recruiter outreach. Includes a second strategy call at the 30-day mark.

*Career services tiers work best when sold to mid-career professionals making a meaningful transition. The 'Full Pivot' tier's 30-day follow-up call is a low-cost add that dramatically increases perceived value and referrals.*

### 8. Business Coaching — Monthly Coaching Tiers

**CLARITY — $500/mo**
2 × 45-minute coaching calls per month. Asynchronous Voxer/voice message support (Mon–Fri, 24-hr response). Access to my resource library.

**MOMENTUM — $1,200/mo**
4 × 60-minute coaching calls per month. Asynchronous Voxer support with same-day response. Monthly action plan document. Review of one business asset (sales page, email, proposal) per month.

**ACCELERATOR — $2,500/mo**
Everything in Momentum + one 90-minute intensive per month for deep-dive strategy, unlimited email support, quarterly business review and 90-day roadmap, direct introductions to two relevant contacts in my network per quarter.

*The Accelerator tier's 'network introductions' is a differentiator that's nearly impossible to commoditize and justifies the price for serious clients. Use this structure when you want to serve clients at multiple investment levels without diluting your high-touch offer.*

### 9. Email Marketing — E-Commerce Brand Setup

**LAUNCH — $1,500**
Klaviyo/Mailchimp account setup, 3 automated flows (welcome series, abandoned cart, post-purchase). Basic email templates (2 designs). Up to 500 existing contacts imported and segmented.

**SCALE — $3,200**
Everything in Launch + 6 automated flows (adds browse abandonment, win-back, and VIP), 5 branded email templates, 2 promotional campaign designs, list segmentation strategy, and 30-day performance review call.

**REVENUE ENGINE — $6,500**
Everything in Scale + 3 months of done-for-you campaign management (8 campaigns/mo), A/B subject line testing, SMS flow setup (3 flows), quarterly strategy sessions, and monthly revenue attribution reporting.

*E-commerce email clients respond to revenue language. The 'Revenue Engine' tier name and the 'revenue attribution reporting' deliverable both speak directly to ROI. Lead with the stat that email drives 30–40% of e-commerce revenue to justify the investment.*

**ESSENTIAL — $395**
60-minute studio session, 1 outfit, 15 fully edited digital images, online gallery for 30 days, standard commercial license.

**SIGNATURE — $695**
2-hour studio or location session, 2 outfits, 30 fully edited digital images, behind-the-scenes reel (30 sec, phone-quality), online gallery for 90 days, extended commercial license.

**BRAND DAY — $1,400**
Full-day session (up to 6 hours), 3 locations, 4 outfits, 75 fully edited images, 2 short-form video clips (edited for Reels/Stories), personal brand strategy pre-call (45 min), online gallery for 1 year, unlimited commercial license.

*The 'Brand Day' tier shifts the framing from 'getting photos' to 'building a visual identity.' The pre-call adds perceived value and improves the final product. Great for coaches, speakers, and consultants refreshing their online presence.*

### 11. Web Development — WordPress Business Site

**LAUNCH — $2,800**
Custom WordPress site, up to 5 pages, mobile-responsive design, contact form, Google Analytics setup, basic SEO configuration, 30-day post-launch support.

**GROWTH — $5,500**
Everything in Launch + up to 10 pages, blog setup with 3 starter posts, speed optimization (Core Web Vitals compliant), integrations (CRM, email marketing tool), 60-day post-launch support, and a 1-hour CMS training call.

**ENTERPRISE — $10,500**
Everything in Growth + up to 20 pages, custom functionality (membership area, booking system, or e-commerce up to 50 products), advanced security setup, 12-month maintenance plan (updates, backups, monitoring), quarterly performance review.

*The 12-month maintenance plan in the Enterprise tier creates recurring revenue. Frame it as 'you're not just buying a website, you're buying a managed digital asset.' Maintenance plans are where long-term client relationships — and stable income — live.*

### 12. PR & Media Outreach — Monthly Retainer

**VISIBILITY — $1,200/mo**
Monthly media audit, 1 press release written and distributed to 50 targeted outlets, 3-month editorial calendar, coverage tracking report.

**INFLUENCE — $2,800/mo**
Everything in Visibility + 2 press releases per month, journalist relationship building (active outreach to 10 contacts/mo), podcast booking pitch to 5 shows/mo, media training document.

**AUTHORITY — $5,500/mo**
Everything in Influence + thought leadership article ghostwriting (1/mo, pitched to tier-1 publications), speaking opportunity research and pitch (3 submissions/mo), crisis communication plan on file, dedicated PR strategist as day-to-day contact.

*PR is hard to quantify, so tiered packaging helps clients understand what they're getting for each price point. The 'Authority' tier's thought leadership ghostwriting is a premium differentiator most boutique PR freelancers don't offer, creating immediate competitive separation.*

### 13. Financial Consulting — CFO Advisory for Startups

**ESSENTIAL CFO — $1,500/mo**
Monthly financial review (P&L, cash flow, balance sheet), one 60-minute advisory call, monthly KPI dashboard (built in Google Sheets or Notion), vendor/tool recommendations.

**STRATEGIC CFO — $3,500/mo**
Everything in Essential + weekly 30-minute check-ins, budget vs. actuals analysis, scenario modeling (3 scenarios per quarter), investor-ready financial narrative updates, and support for one fundraising or banking conversation per quarter.

**FRACTIONAL CFO — $7,500/mo**
Everything in Strategic + unlimited advisory access (Slack + email), board meeting financial preparation, cap table advisory, due diligence preparation, team management support for bookkeeper or controller, and annual strategic financial plan.

*Fractional CFO packaging is in high demand among Series A and pre-Series A startups. The key is showing what you're NOT included in lower tiers (e.g., board support, due diligence) so that the top tier feels genuinely differentiated, not just 'more hours.'*

### 14. Interior Design — Residential E-Design

**ROOM REFRESH — $750**
One room. Mood board, furniture + decor sourcing list with clickable links, color palette, and one layout diagram. 48-hour response window on questions for 2 weeks post-delivery.

**FULL ROOM DESIGN — $1,800**
One room. Everything in Refresh + 2 layout options, 3D rendering (1 view), styling guide, shopping list with tiered budget options (budget/mid/splurge), and two revision rounds.

**HOME TRANSFORMATION — $5,500**
Up to 4 rooms. Everything in Full Room Design per room + cohesive whole-home color story, contractor/trade vendor recommendations, virtual installation walkthrough call, and 30-day post-project styling support.

*E-design packages democratize interior design without requiring physical presence. The 'Home Transformation' tier works well for clients who just bought a home. Always emphasize the time savings vs. doing it themselves — design indecision is the real pain point.*

### 15. Podcast Production — Done-For-You Monthly

**AUDIO ONLY — $600/mo**
Up to 4 episodes/month. Audio editing and mixing, intro/outro music integration, show notes (150 words/episode), upload to your podcast host, chapter markers.

**FULL PRODUCTION — $1,200/mo**
Everything in Audio Only + audiogram (1 per episode for social), episode transcript, guest coordination emails, podcast SEO (title + description optimization), and monthly listener growth report.

**GROWTH ENGINE — $2,500/mo**
Everything in Full Production + repurposed short-form video clips (3 per episode), social media caption set (3 captions per episode), quarterly podcast strategy call, guest outreach (5 pitches/mo), and Apple Podcasts/Spotify ad creative for one campaign.

*Podcast production clients often underestimate how much work goes into growth beyond recording. The 'Growth Engine' tier reframes your service as audience building, not just editing — which justifies the price jump and resonates with hosts who feel stuck at the same listener count.*

### 16. HR Consulting — Small Business People Ops

**COMPLIANCE FOUNDATION — $2,200 (one-time)**
Employee handbook creation (up to 30 policies), offer letter template, I-9 and onboarding checklist, 2-hour compliance walkthrough call. For companies hiring their first 1–5 employees.

**PEOPLE OPS SETUP — $5,500 (one-time)**
Everything in Compliance Foundation + job description library (up to 10 roles), interview question guide, performance review template, compensation banding framework (up to 3 levels), and HRIS tool recommendation + basic setup.

**FRACTIONAL HR PARTNER — $2,000/mo (retainer)**
Ongoing HR advisory: monthly strategy call, unlimited HR question support (Slack/email), management coaching on sticky employee situations, quarterly policy updates, and support through one hiring cycle per quarter (job posting review, offer structuring, onboarding checklist).

*This structure works uniquely well because it has two one-time project tiers plus a retainer option. Presenting all three naturally leads clients to ask about the retainer, especially after they've completed the setup work. Keeps revenue predictable for you.*

### 17. Content Strategy — B2B SaaS

**BLUEPRINT — $3,500 (one-time)**
Content audit of existing assets, ICP content journey mapping, keyword + topic cluster research (50 topics), 6-month editorial calendar, channel prioritization recommendation. Delivered as a ready-to-execute strategy document.

**STRATEGY + EXECUTION — $5,500/mo**
Everything in Blueprint (month 1) + 4 long-form articles per month, internal linking strategy implemented, content distribution playbook, monthly SEO performance review, and one content repurposing guide per quarter.

**FULL CONTENT DEPARTMENT — $12,000/mo**
Everything in Strategy + Execution + 8 long-form articles, 2 gated lead magnets per quarter, email newsletter (4 issues/mo), LinkedIn thought leadership posts for one executive (12/mo), and fractional content director access for team management.

*The 'Full Content Department' tier positions you as a replacement for an in-house hire, which reframes the cost comparison entirely. B2B SaaS companies know a senior content director costs $130K+/year — $144K annually suddenly sounds reasonable for a full team output.*
