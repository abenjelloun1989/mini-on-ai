## Detailed Description (5000 chars max — paste as-is)

**Free Gmail invoice tracker Chrome extension — never miss an overdue payment**

InvoiceGuard adds a one-click "Track Invoice" button directly into Gmail. AI reads any invoice email and auto-fills client name, amount, invoice date, and due date. Your dashboard shows everything that's outstanding, overdue, and paid — without leaving your inbox.

**How it works:**
1. Open any invoice email in Gmail — InvoiceGuard adds a "📋 Track Invoice" button automatically
2. Click the button — AI extracts client, amount, invoice date, and due date in seconds. Edit any field, then save.
3. Your dashboard shows all tracked invoices. Overdue ones are flagged red. One click generates an AI follow-up reminder and opens Gmail compose pre-filled.

**What you get:**
✓ Gmail-native tracking — track directly from any invoice email, no copy-pasting
✓ AI field extraction — Claude reads the email and auto-fills all invoice details in seconds
✓ Overdue alerts — badge icon shows overdue count; dashboard flags late payments in red
✓ AI follow-up reminders — generate friendly, professional, or firm reminders in one click, pre-filled in Gmail compose
✓ Payment dashboard — see total outstanding, overdue count, and all invoices by status
✓ CSV export — export everything for your accountant or tax return (Pro)

**Built for:**
– Freelancers with multiple active clients
– Independent contractors tracking project payments
– Consultants who invoice via email
– Small agency owners chasing receivables

**Free plan:** Track up to 5 active invoices with AI extraction and overdue alerts. No account needed.

**Pro plan ($7/month):** Unlimited invoice tracking + AI-personalized follow-up reminders (friendly / professional / firm tone) + CSV export + analytics dashboard.

**Privacy:** InvoiceGuard uses a randomly generated anonymous ID. We do not collect your name or email address. Invoice email text is processed by AI to extract fields and then discarded — we do not store raw email content. Invoice metadata (client name, amount, dates, status) is stored securely linked to your anonymous ID. We never sell or share your data.
